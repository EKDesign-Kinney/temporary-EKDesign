The Most Important Question: Why?

Evan Kinney Design is based in the Blue Mountains, New South Wales, specialising in corporate design such as logos, business cards, letterheads, posters, flyers and web pages.

What matters most is the reason for doing business. There is a lot of bad and ineffective graphic design out there, and many cheap or even free options on the market to achieve that. While those tools have their place, nothing can beat the expertise of a trained professional.

With attention to the brand identity, what the client's company stands for and what specifications are important to the client, Evan Kinney Design offers and up-market, personalised alternative to cheap design which often tragically falls short of its intended purpose.
